# BluePrintAI Reviewer Test Instructions

Production app URL: `https://blueprintai-app.onrender.com`

1. Install BluePrintAI on the development store.
2. Open the embedded app in Shopify Admin and complete onboarding if prompted.
3. Go to **Connections**.
4. Google Ads is optional and read-only. A connected test account may show 0 live rows when it has no active/reportable campaigns.
5. Click **Load demo Google Ads data** to create clearly labeled local demo reporting rows. This action does not write to Google Ads.
6. Go to **Command Center**.
7. Confirm **Ad & campaign effectiveness** shows the demo performance rows rather than an empty state.
8. Confirm demo rows say: **“Demo data — not from your live Google Ads account.”**
9. Visit Creative Library, AI Review Studio, Recommendations, Ad Briefs, Revenue Blueprint, Creators, Data Import, Campaigns, Connections, Settings, Privacy, Terms, Support, and Data Deletion.
10. Confirm TikTok Ads and Meta Ads are marked **Coming soon** and manual CSV import remains available.
11. Confirm campaign records are local planning folders only. No campaigns are created, edited, launched, paused, funded, or charged on an ad platform. Google Ads access is reporting-only.
12. Return to Connections and click **Clear demo Google Ads data**. Confirm only demo rows disappear and Google Ads remains connected.

If live Google Ads access is not available to the reviewer, the rest of the app and the local demo-data walkthrough remain testable without it.
