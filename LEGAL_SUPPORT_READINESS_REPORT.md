# Legal & Support Readiness Report

## Framework and router

- React 18 application using React Router 7 framework mode.
- File-system routes are generated by `@react-router/fs-routes` from `app/routes.js`.
- Shopify-authenticated pages use the `/app/*` route namespace; public legal/support pages use root routes.

## Initial inventory

| Page | Public route | Embedded route | Initial state |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Privacy Policy | `/privacy` | `/app/privacy` | Both existed and rendered shared content. Content included a hard-coded email, address, effective date, entity claims, and unverifiable legal/retention language. |
| Terms of Service | `/terms` | `/app/terms` | Both existed and rendered shared content. Content included hard-coded owner details, governing-law/venue choices, and unverified binding legal clauses. |
| Support | `/support` | `/app/support` | Both existed and were reachable. Both hard-coded a personal email address. |

The landing footer initially linked Privacy, Terms, and Support. The shared legal footer linked Privacy, Terms, Contact, and ancillary policies, but not Support or Data Deletion. The authenticated sidebar did not link the required legal/support pages.
